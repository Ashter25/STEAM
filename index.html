This is the Short History of Eco-Enzyme

A researcher named Dr. Rosukon Poompanvong discovered a solution, which she then called the Eco-enzyme. Dr. Poompanvong has suffered from a blood disease since birth, which made her determined to find a way to help other people. This led to the creation of the eco-enzyme, originally called trash enzyme.
